Sumilon Island

Located off the southeastern coast of Cebu, Sumilon Island is a pristine paradise renowned for its crystal-clear waters, powdery white sand beaches, and vibrant marine biodiversity. Managed by the Bluewater Resorts Group, Sumilon Island is a protected marine sanctuary and eco-tourism destination that offers visitors a tranquil escape into nature's embrace.

White Sand Beaches

Sumilon Island is blessed with some of the most beautiful beaches in the Philippines, featuring soft, powdery white sands that stretch for kilometers along its coastline. The island's main attraction is its iconic sandbar, a narrow strip of sand that emerges during low tide, creating a stunning contrast against the azure waters of the surrounding sea. Visitors can lounge on the beach, swim in the clear waters, or snorkel among colorful coral reefs teeming with marine life.

Marine Sanctuary

Sumilon Island is home to a thriving marine sanctuary, where coral reefs, seagrass beds, and mangrove forests provide a vital habitat for a diverse array of marine species. Snorkeling and diving enthusiasts can explore the island's underwater wonders, encountering colorful fish, vibrant corals, and fascinating sea creatures such as sea turtles, reef sharks, and rays. Guided snorkeling tours and diving excursions are available for visitors of all experience levels, allowing them to explore the rich biodiversity of the marine sanctuary.

Nature Trails and Eco-Adventures

In addition to its stunning beaches and underwater attractions, Sumilon Island offers a variety of eco-adventures and nature trails for visitors to explore. Hiking trails wind through the island's interior, leading to viewpoints that offer panoramic vistas of the surrounding seascape and lush tropical landscapes. Birdwatchers can spot a variety of avian species in the island's forests, while nature lovers can learn about the island's flora and fauna through guided eco-tours and educational programs.

Luxury Eco-Resort

Sumilon Island is home to the luxurious Bluewater Sumilon Island Resort, an eco-friendly retreat that blends seamlessly with its natural surroundings. The resort offers a range of upscale accommodations, including beachfront villas, hilltop cottages, and rustic glamping tents, all designed to provide guests with a comfortable and immersive island experience. Guests can indulge in gourmet dining, pampering spa treatments, and a variety of recreational activities, including kayaking, paddleboarding, and island-hopping tours.

Conservation and Sustainability

Sumilon Island is committed to environmental conservation and sustainable tourism practices, with efforts focused on protecting its delicate marine ecosystem and promoting responsible visitor behavior. The island's marine sanctuary is strictly protected, with rules and regulations in place to minimize human impact on the environment. Conservation initiatives such as reef monitoring, mangrove reforestation, and waste management programs help preserve the island's natural beauty for future generations to enjoy.
